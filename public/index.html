What OGZPrime Is

OGZPrime is an automated trading system designed to operate continuously under real market conditions, with behavior that is observed, recorded, and verified over time.

Order Handling

Orders can be placed, validated, canceled, and reconciled without duplication.

Restart Behavior

The system can restart without corrupting state or re-issuing trades.

Continuous Operation

Runtime behavior is tracked day-by-day, not reconstructed after the fact.

Instance Isolation

Test and customer instances operate without touching production data.

Why This Exists

OGZPrime was built to solve one problem: trading systems that work in testing often fail in production. This system was designed from the start to be monitored, recorded, and verified under real conditions — not reconstructed after the fact.
